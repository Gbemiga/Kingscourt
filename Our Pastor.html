THE PASTOR

Pastor Segun Gbadebo (PSG) is the Parish Pastor of RCCG Kingâ€™s Court, Dublin and the Zonal Pastor of RCCG Zone 7, Republic of Ireland.

He is known for his practical and realistic presentation of the Word of God, a motivational speaker, who is passionate about restoring the broken walls in the life of people. He believes in doing unusual things for the unusual God that brings about unusual miracles.

The central theme of his message is achieving great height in spite of contrary past with emphasis on addressing the critical pain processes and principle of activating a great destiny without losing the focus of the kingdom. A great encourager as he inspires you to be the best in God.

Pastor Segun is a trained Computer Scientist from Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile Ife, Nigeria and currently works as an I.T. Consultant. He is happily married to his long standing friend, Titilayo and they are blessed with three children, Esther Idunnuoluwa, David Tijesunimi and Eunice Oluwadarasimi. He is the author of "Marriage : Practical Questions, Practical Answers".

Pastor Segun plays golf for leisure and he is also a lover of football, a supporter of Chelsea Football Club, UK and Nigerian National Team (Super Eagles).
